About me

I’m a graphic designer with experience in creating both digital and print visuals - from advertising banners and Times Square billboards to landing pages, websites, and branded merchandise. I work with Adobe Photoshop, Illustrator and Figma, and integrate AI tools like MidJourney and Firefly to generate modern, bold and creative solutions.

I collaborate closely with marketing teams, helping brands strengthen their online and offline presence. My work includes banners for websites and social media, promotional graphics for campaigns, and designing merchandise prepared for print. I combine attention to detail, design trends, and business-oriented thinking to deliver visuals that work.

My expertise spans graphic design and web design: I design landing pages and websites, work on redesigns, and create responsive layouts that balance aesthetics and functionality. My goal is to craft designs that are clear, engaging and support business growth.
